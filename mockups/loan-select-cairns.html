Our story

Built on bank-grade experience

Loan Select Cairns started in May 2000, after David left Westpac with the closing of his last branch. Drawing on extensive bank training across mortgages, settlements, loan legals and approvals, he set out to help customers navigate the home-loan process and reach a good outcome.

More than two decades on, we're still a centrally located Cairns family business — the kind you can sit across the table from.
